First Aid Guidance

Follow these steps immediately. Do not panic.

1. CPR Steps

If unresponsive and not breathing normally.

  • check_circle Push hard and fast in the center of the chest (100-120 pushes a minute).
  • air Tilt head back slightly, lift chin, and give 2 rescue breaths if trained.
  • warning Continue until help arrives or they wake up.
water_drop

2. Bleeding Control

For severe bleeding from wounds.

  • front_hand Apply firm, direct pressure on the wound with a clean cloth.
  • compress Keep pressing continuously. Do not remove the cloth to check.
  • arrow_upward If on an arm or leg, elevate the limb above the heart if possible.
